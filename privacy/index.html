Privacy Policy — built around a simple promise: your data stays on your device.

Privacy Policy · a labidi.eu project

GHOSTTOOTH is developed with a commitment to user privacy and transparency. We do not track you, we do not collect personal data, and we do not use analytics. The application runs entirely on your device and is fully functional offline.

This policy explains — in plain language — what the app does and does not do with your information, and the one optional situation in which any data leaves your device.

02 Data we collect

No collection

We do not collect, store, or share any user data.

GHOSTTOOTH operates entirely on your device with no backend tracking mechanisms. Bluetooth scan results, device lists, signal readings, and any notes you make stay local to your phone. They are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

Specifically, the app does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, account IDs)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, crash telemetry, or behavioural data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or other content on your device
  • The results of your Bluetooth scans

03 Network activity

The app works without a network — but the first launch reaches out once, if it can.

GHOSTTOOTH does not need a network connection to work. The first time you run the app, it will — automatically, if a network connection is possible — fetch the latest device definitions (the reference lists used to recognise device makers and signal types) from our servers.

If no network is available, the app still works and scans normally; it simply cannot recognise what each detected device is until it is able to fetch these definitions.

Full transparency

When the app fetches definitions, your device connects to our server over the internet. As with any web request, our server can momentarily see your device’s public IP address. This is a technical necessity of any internet connection, not something we track or profile.

We do not use this IP address to identify you, build a profile, or link requests together. No personal data and no scan results are ever sent.

What is sentA standard request for the current definition files. No personal data or scan results are included.
What is visibleYour device’s public IP address, inherent to any internet connection.
PurposeSolely to deliver the device definitions used for recognition.
WhenOn first launch, automatically if a network is possible, and thereafter to refresh definitions. The app runs fully without it.
Legal basis (GDPR)Our legitimate interest in delivering the definitions that make the app useful (Art. 6(1)(f)).

04 Offline functionality

GHOSTTOOTH is designed to work completely offline. All core features — scanning for nearby Bluetooth devices, reading signal strength, and reviewing results — are available without any internet connection. Server connectivity is optional and used only for definition updates at your discretion.

05 Device permissions

Why the app asks for what it asks for.

To scan for nearby Bluetooth devices, Android requires the app to request certain permissions. These are used only for the app’s scanning function and are processed on your device.

Bluetooth / Nearby devicesRequired to discover and read advertisements from nearby Bluetooth devices. This is the app’s core function.
Location (if requested)On many Android versions, the operating system ties Bluetooth scanning to location permission. The app does not collect, store, or transmit your location — this permission exists solely because Android requires it to enable Bluetooth scanning.

The app does not request access to your contacts, photos, files, camera, or microphone.

06 Third parties & sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, because we do not collect it in the first place. GHOSTTOOTH contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no social media trackers.

The application is distributed through the Google Play Store. Your interactions with the store listing itself (such as installing or reviewing the app) are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy, not this one.

07 Children’s privacy

GHOSTTOOTH does not collect personal data from anyone, including children. The app is not directed at children, and because no data is collected, there is nothing gathered from users of any age.

08 Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) grants you rights over your personal data — including the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.

Because GHOSTTOOTH does not collect or store any personal data on our servers, we hold no personal information about you to access, correct, export, or delete. Any data the app generates (such as your scan history) lives only on your device and is fully under your control; uninstalling the app or clearing its data removes it entirely.
